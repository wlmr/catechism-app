Frequently Asked Questions

How does the mastery system work?
Each question progresses through three mastery levels: flashcard (read the answer), multiple choice (choose from options), and written (type the answer from memory). You must answer correctly three times at the written level to fully master a question. A lesson is marked complete only when all its questions are fully mastered.
What is the Review tab for?
The Review tab uses spaced repetition (the SM-2 algorithm) to resurface questions you've already encountered in lessons. Questions appear at increasing intervals — answering correctly pushes the next review further out; answering incorrectly brings it back sooner. Only questions you've previously attempted in a lesson session will appear here.
Does the app work offline?
Yes — all content and your progress are stored on-device. No internet connection is required after the initial app download. Sign in with Apple is optional; if you skip it, your data remains local only.
What is Sign in with Apple used for?
Sign in with Apple is entirely optional. It creates a simple account identifier on your device that can be used for future cloud sync features. If you sign in, no personal data is sent to any external server.
Can I reset my progress?
Yes. Go to Profile → Settings → Reset Progress. This clears all mastery levels and review history. The action cannot be undone, so a confirmation prompt will appear before anything is deleted.
What is Parent Mode?
Parent Mode is a biometric-protected area where you can quiz your children and track their progress separately from your own. Access it from the Profile tab. You'll need Face ID or Touch ID to enter and exit.
Which edition of the Catechism is used?
The app covers all three numbered editions of the Baltimore Catechism (No. 1, No. 2, and No. 3) plus a Prayers course. Psalm citations follow the Douay-Rheims (Vulgate) numbering. Explanations and notes are sourced from primary texts: Scripture, the Church Fathers, the Roman Catechism, and St. Thomas Aquinas.
The app isn't syncing between my devices. Why?
Cross-device sync is not available in the current version — all data is stored locally on the device you use. If you reinstall the app or switch devices, progress will start fresh. We plan to add iCloud sync in a future update.
I found a content error. How do I report it?
Please email us at wilmernilsson@gmail.com with the lesson name, question, and a description of the error. We take accuracy seriously and will review every report promptly.
